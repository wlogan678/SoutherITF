» Three Step Sparring (Without a Partner)
Watch Video
A clear understanding of the purpose of three step sparring is best developed from white belt stage.
 
Purpose:
  1. To be able to determine the correct angle and distance for attack and defence techniques
  2. To maintain correct stances with the appropriate placement and distance between the feet
  3. To gain an understanding of the correct tools to be used for attache and defence
NOTE: All white belt 3 step sparring is performed without a partner.
» Routine #1
X as attacker

A) Take distance in walking stance.
B) step back into left walking stance low block (kyup)
C) attack using 3 mid section front punches in walking stance
D) step right foot into chunbi
 
Y as defender

A) (kyup) step back in walking stance with inner block 3 times
B) counter attack with reverse punch using sine wave
C) step right foor into chunbi
 
» Routine #2
X

C) Three front snap kicks with reaction force followed by twin side block
D) Step right foot into chunbi
 
Y

B) Counter with front leg snap kick followed by low block same side of body.
C) step right foot into chunbi
