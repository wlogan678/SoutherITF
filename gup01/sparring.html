» Sparring
Advanced three step sparring
Students are required to devise three advanced level examples and demonstrate these attacking and defending in both directions.
 
Model Sparring
Students should devise three examples against different hand attacks and three examples against different foot attacks. These should all reflect the appropriate attacking / defending tools and angles and distances for attack and defence.
 
Foot technique sparring
Students will be expected to demonstrate foot technique sparring against a single opponent for several different opponents
 
Free Sparring
One two minute round against two opponents and then nine two minute rounds against a single opponent. The student will be given a thirty second break between opponents.
