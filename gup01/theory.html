Interpretation of Choong-Moo
Choong-Moo was the given name to the great Admiral Yi Soon-Sin of the Yi Dynasty. He was reputed top have invented the first armoured battleship (kobukson) which was the precursor of the present day submarine in 1592 A.D. The reason why this pattern ends up with the left hand attack is to symbolise his regrettable death having no chance to show his unrestrained potential by the forced reservation of his loyalty to the king.
Movements: 30
Diagram: "I"
End: Left Foot Moves Back
 
Meaning of belt
Black, Opposite of White, therefore signifying the maturity and proficiency in TaeKwon-Do. It also indicates the wearer's imperviousness to darkness and fear
