» One Step Sparring
Watch Video
Using Yul-Gok as a basis.
 
Start from attention stance. On command of 'One Step Sparring Ready', X moves right foot to parallel stance, Y moves left foot.
 
Note: Free sparring is also required.
» Routine #1
X

(A) From parallel stance move into right walking stance with high punch
 
Y

(A) Step back into left walking stance with palm hooking block
(B) Pulling partner close perform reverse punch
» Routine #2
X

(A) From parallel stance perform mid front punch in right walking stance
 
Y

(A) Step back into left walking stance with double forearm block
(B) On the spot in walking stance, perform mid right straight fingertip strike
» Routine #3
X

(A) From parallel stance perform front punch in right walking stance
 
Y

(A) Step rear foot one step to right and form Right Leg Bending Stance Type A
(B) Perform left side piercing kick. Return to L Stance guarding block
» Routine #4
X

(A) From parallel stance perform front punch in right walking stance
 
Y

(A) Step back into left walking stance with left inner block
(B) Step into x stance with side backfist strike.
» Routine #5
X

(A) Right foot middle side front snap kick
 
Y

(A) Step back into left L stance and use a crescent kick with right foot
(B) Jump into X stance side backfist strike
» Routine #6
X

(A) Right foot middle side piercing kick
 
Y

(A) Right L stance with reverse inwards outer forearm block
(B) Pivot on left foot 180 degrees clockwise into left rear foot stance with right knife hand side strike.

» Semi Free Sparring
Watch Video
At the command of Semi Free Sparring Ready:
 
X and Y take a left or right L stance with a forearm or knife-hand guarding block.
» Routine #1
(X) Low front snap kick with the right foot.
(Y) Moving right foot back into left walking stance low block
(X) Step down with right front punch.
(Y) Right L stance forearm guarding block, dodging to B (backwards)
(Y) Low turning kick with right foot
(X) Right L stance knife hand guarding block, moving right foot to A.
(Y) Low side piercing kick with the left foot to floating ribs
(X) Floating ribs are attacked.
 
» Routine #2
(X) Right high obverse punch with right fist
(Y) Left walking stance outer forearm obverse block
(Y) Low front snap kick with right foot.
(X) Right L stance forearm guarding block moving right foot to A
(X) Low front snap kick with left foot
(Y) Right walking stance X-fist pressing block
(X) Right L stance middle reverse punch with the left fist
(Y) Philtrum is attacked
