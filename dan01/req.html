» Requirements for 2nd Dan Grading
Stances
Diagonal Stance
Crouched Stance
One Leg Stance
Parallel stance with heaven hand
 
Fundamental movements
Walking stance upset punch
Walking stance palm upward block
Walking stance double forearm block followed by low reverse block
Walking stance double arc hand high block
Low stance flat fingertip high obverse thrust
Low stance flat fingertip high reverse thrust
Forward double stepping walking stance palm hooking block
L stance U shaped grasp
L stance middle knuckle fist middle punch
Sitting stance angle punch
Sitting stance forefist pressing block
Sitting stance inner forearm wedging block
Sitting stance back elbow thrust
Sitting stance horizontal punch
Sitting stance back fist side back strike
Sitting stance reverse knife hand low guarding block
Sitting stance scooping block
Sitting stance 9 shaped block
Sitting stance knife hand low guarding block
X stance forearm low front block with palm to side fist
X stance forearm low front block with wrist to finger belly
X stance double forearm high block
Close stance side fist downwards strike
Close stance knife hand low front block
Close stance consecutive side kicking
Close stance twin elbow horizontal thrust
 
Kicks
Any kick from previous grades
Also a selection of the following should be demonstrated
Flying double side kick vertically
Flying double side kick horizontally
Flying double turning kick vertically
Flying double turning kick horizontally
Flying double front kick vertically
Flying double turning kick horizontally
Flying twisting kick
Flying front back kick (front kick and back piercing kick)
Flying side twisting kick (twisting kick and side piercing kick)
Flying consecutive kicks
Flying spiral kicks (side and side or side and back)
Over 35 yr olds: A selection of consecutive kicks and combination kicks from previous grades
 
 Patterns
Kwang Gae
Po Eun
Gae Baek
 
Sparring
Three step with a partner one and two way
Two step with a partner
One step with a partner
Semi free sparring with a partner
Model sparring
Foot technique sparring
Free sparring
 
Self defence
Defence against single attacker both unarmed and armed in standing, sitting and lying position
Defence against multiple attackers both unarmed and armed
 
Power Breaks
Junior: Junior boards - Two boards knifehand strike, 3 breaks with feet to two junior boards other than those for first degree
 
Women: Senior boards - Two boards hand technique of choice (from standing position), Three boards downwards knifehand strike, 3 breaks with feet to 2 boards other than those for first degree
 
Men: Senior board - Three board hand technique of choice (from standing position), Downwards knifehand strike to handmade brick, 3 breaks with feet to 3 boards other than those for first degree
 
Focus Breaks
Junior: Junior board - A selection of three techniques from kicking requirements
 
Women: Junior board - A selection of three techniques from kicking requirements
 
Men: Senior board - A selection of three techniques from kicking requirements
 
Speed Breaks
Junior: Junior board - Suspended single board turning kick, suspended single board hand technique
 
Women: Junior board - Suspended single board turning kick, suspended single board hand technique
 
Men: Senior board - Suspended single board turning kick, suspended single board hand technique
 

 
Theory
Any questions from Encyclopaedia including terminology
Adults: A two thousand word essay on the responsibility and attitude required of instructors.
Juniors: A thousand word essay on why you wish to be a 2nd Degree black belt and what it means.
 
Additional requirements
A demonstrated commitment to the art of Taekwon-Do as judged by the Senior instructor.
 
This should include assisting in instructing, promotion of the club and the ITF and some form of participation in Tournaments, either as competitor or official.
