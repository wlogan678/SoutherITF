Interpretation of Hwa-rang
Hwa-rang is named after the Hwa-Rang youth group which originated in the Silla Dynasty in the early 7th Century. The 29 movements refer to the 29th infantry Division, when Taekwon-Do developed into maturity.
Movements: 29
Diagram: "I"
End: Right Foot Moves Back
 
Meaning of Belt
Red signifies danger, cautioning students to exercise control and warning the opponent to stay away.
 
Hwa-rang Warrior Code
  • Be Loyal to your King
  • Be obedient to your parents
  • Be honourable to your friends
  • Never retreat in battle
  • Make a just Kill
Three Kingdoms of Korea
