7th Gup's are required to demonstrated advanced three step sparring with a partner and two step sparring using Do-San as a basis.
 
Note: Simple controlled technique free sparring is also required.
» Three Step Sparring - Routine #1
X as attacker

A) Step back into left walking stance low block (kyup)
B) Step forwards with L-Stance middle reverse punch.

 
Y as defender

A) L stance reverse knife hand block 3 times. Finish with middle side piercing kick with left foot

 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
» Routine #2
X as attacker

A) Step back into left walking stance low block (kyup)
B) Step forwards with fixed stance middle reverse punch.

 
Y as defender

A) Walking stance reverse outer forearm block. Finish with middle side piercing kick with right foot

 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
» Routine #3
X as attacker

A) Step back into left walking stance low block (kyup)
B) Step forwards with L-Stance middle reverse punch.

 
Y as defender

A) L-Stance inward forearm block. Finish with a left leg middle side piercing kick

 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
» Two Step Sparring
Using Do-San as a basis.
 
Partners face each other at appropriate distance for attack in attention stance. At the command 'two step sparring':
» Two Step Sparring - Routine #1
X

A) Steps back into right L stance guarding block (kyup)
B) Steps forward with high front punch in walking stance
C) Steps forward executing rear foot front snap kick.

 
Y

A) Moves into parallel stance then indicates readiness with kyup then steps back into left walking stance with high outer block
B) Step into left L stance low block.
C) Moving lead leg step into sitting stance perform knife hand side strike.

 
Both partners return to parallel stance moving right leg.
» Routine #2
X

A) Steps back into right L stance guarding block (kyup)
B) steps forward with front snap kick from rear foot
C) Steps forward with grab for throat

 
Y

A) Step back into left walking stance low block
B) Step back into right walking stance wedging block
C) Finish by stepping right leg to left and then perform left snap kick then double punch.
