The 9th Gup is now expected to perform the white belt routine with a partner.
 
In walking stance, right toes should be alongside partners left toes.
 
To take distance in #2 and #3: In walking stance, right heel should be halfway alongside partner's left foot.
 
Note: Whilst stepping forward, foot moves outside partner's, inside partners, outside partners.
