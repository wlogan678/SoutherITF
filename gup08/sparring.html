» 3 Step Sparring (Two Way)
Watch Video
Using Dan-Gun as a basis
» Routine #1
X as attacker

A) Take distance in walking stance by placing right heel alongside partners left heel.
B) Step back with low block in walking stance, then three mid punches in walking stance.

 
Y as defender

A) Right L stance knife hand guarding block followed by two more
B) Lead leg front snap kick followed by knife-hand guarding block

 
On the command of continue

 
X) Moves backwards in walking stance with a high punch 3 times
Y) Moves forwards in L stance with three knife hand guarding blocks and then a front leg low snap kick followed by knife hand guarding block.
 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
» Routine #2
X as attacker

A) Take distance in walking stance.
B) Step back with low block in walking stance.
C) Three high punches in walking stance
 
Y as defender

A) Three high blocks in walking stance
B) Finish with reverse high punch

 
On the command of continue

 
X) Moves backwards with three high punches in walking stance
Y) Moves forwards with three rising blocks finishing with reverse punch
 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
» Routine #3
X as attacker

A) Take distance in walking stance.
B) Step back with low block in walking stance.
C) Three mid punches in walking stance
 
Y as defender

A) Three twin outer forearm blocks in L-stance
B) Finish with knife hand side strike

 
On the command of continue

 
X) Moves backwards with three mid punches in walking stance
Y) Moves forwards with three twin outer forearm blocks finishing with knife hand strike
 
X and Y then return to parallel ready stance.
Repeat with Y as attacker
